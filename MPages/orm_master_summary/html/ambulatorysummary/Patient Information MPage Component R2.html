Overview

The Patient Information component displays the patient's chief complaint, primary physician, emergency contact and phone number, and advanced directive.

Note
As an example, chief complaint and advanced directive can be populated from the Ambulatory Intake and Admission History PowerForms.

Positioning the mouse over the emergency phone number displays any additional phone numbers for that contact. Clicking the section header takes you to the Patient Information component in the chart. You can go back to the Ambulatory Summary screen by clicking in the Table of Contents. Most of the patient information fields are populated based on registration information, but the chief complaint and advanced directive come from clinical documentation.

Component Details

Heading

  • Patient Information
  • Component heading links to appropriate component in the chart.

Content

  • Chief Complaint: This is a free text comment of what the patient states is their reason for this visit.
  • Primary Physician: - Primary Physician's name
  • Emergency Contact: Emergency Contact's name
  • Emergency #: Emergency Contact's phone number
  • Advance Directive: Displays the last documented Advance Directive result.

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: pull historical data from lifetime and current information from admission data
  • Truncate / Wrap: text wraps if it exceeds the column width
  • Scrolling: None

Hover

  • Hovering over Emergency # will display all phone numbers for the emergency contact
