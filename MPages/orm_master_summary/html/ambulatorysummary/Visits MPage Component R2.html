MPages Help Pages : Visits MPage Component R2
This page last changed on Aug 17, 2010 by RP018820.

The Visits component displays the previous five patient visits, and up to five future visits. The previous visits are displayed first and in reverse chronological order, followed by any future visits in chronological order. Hovering over a visit displays the visit reason.

Clicking the Visits section header takes you to the Patient Demographic component in the chart where you can look at the Visit List and view additional information. Then return to the summary page by clicking in the Table of Contents.
Component Details

Heading

  • Visits (n)
    n = total number of previous visits and total number of future visits
  • Component heading links to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Visits are grouped into sub-headings of Previous and Future
  • Date of visit: Format = MM/DD/YY
  • Type of visit: Encounter
  • Location of visit

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: Previous 5 visits and up to 5 future visits
  • Sort Order: display past visits first in reverse chronological order, then display future visits in chronological order
  • Truncate / Wrap: text wraps if it exceeds the column width
  • Scrolling: client-defined

Hover

  • Visit Reason: followed by the reason for the visit.
