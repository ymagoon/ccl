In the Ambulatory Summary MPage the end user is able to rearrange components on the page and define the expand/collapse default for each component. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click the Customize View link in the upper right-hand corner.
  2. Left-click a component and drag it to its new location.
    Note
    You change your layout from a three column view to a two column view by moving everything over into the first two columns. Components that have a plus sign default to being collapsed, and components with a negative sign default to being expanded. Change the default by clicking on the icon.
  3. When you have made your changes, click on Save Preferences.
