MPages Help Pages : Overview of Pregnancy Summary
This page last changed on Apr 12, 2010 by MB9512.

The Pregnancy Summary Mpage displays patient specific information related to an active pregnancy (for patients with the encounter type of Antepartum). You can use this view in place of the current functionality provided by the Win32 Pregnancy Summary tab and Pregnancy GenView in PowerChart(PowerChart.exe).

The components of this Mpage display all social history items for the lifetime of the patient. Hovering over an item will display the name of the category and any details that were documented. You can view the patient's social history from the Social History tab in History control (in the chart). Click on a component header to open the History control in the chart (if setup in the Bedrock Wizard).
