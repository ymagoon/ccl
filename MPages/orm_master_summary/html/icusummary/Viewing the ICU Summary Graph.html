MPages Help Pages : Viewing the ICU Summary Graph
This page last changed on Jul 26, 2010 by JP3026.

You can view the trends for various respiratory therapy monitoring categories (like Respiratory Rate, Oral Temperature, Oxygen Flow Rate, Mean Arterial Pressure, etc.) using the ICU Summary MPage Graph view. Also, you can change the data-points displayed on the graphs. For more information, see the topic Selecting the Range on the ICU Summary Graph. Follow the instructions below to display the ICU Summary Graph view.

  1. Once you have displayed a patient in PowerChartÂ®, click the ICU Summary MPage tab on the menu. The ICU Summary MPage is displayed to the right of the PowerChart menu.
  2. At the top of the ICU Summary MPage, click the plus sign on the Respiratory Graph component to fully expand it. The system displays the master graph, which contains a range for a seven day time period, and it displays the two subordinate graphs in the component view.
  3. For information about using the zoom feature and modifying the data-points displayed on the graph, see the topic Selecting the Range on the ICU Summary Graph.
