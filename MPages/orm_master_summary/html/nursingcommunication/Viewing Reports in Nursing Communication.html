MPages Help Pages : Viewing Reports in Nursing Communication
This page last changed on Apr 12, 2010 by MB9512.

Microbiology

To view Microbiology Reports from the Microbiology component in the Nursing Communication MPage select the date and time of the report you want to view. The MPage opens the report. Close the report when you are finished reviewing the patient information.

Pathology

To view Pathology Reports from the Pathology component in the Nursing Communication MPage select the date and time of the result you want to view. The time in this case represents the time elapsed since the result was documented. The MPage opens the diagnostic report. Close the report when you are finished reviewing the patient information.

Diagnostics

To view Diagnostics Reports from the Diagnostics component in the Nursing Communication MPage select the date and time of the result you want to view. The time in this case represents the time elapsed since the result was documented. The MPage opens the Clinical Notes Viewer to display the diagnostic report and any appropriate images in the report (for example, scanned documents). Close the report when you are finished reviewing the patient information.
