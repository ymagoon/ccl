MPages Help Pages : Viewing Graphs in Nursing Communication
This page last changed on Aug 14, 2011 by JP3026.

Vital Signs
To view individual numeric lab information from the Vital Signs component in the Nursing Communication MPages view as a graph, select the name of the vital sign you want to view. Close the graph when you are finished reviewing the patient information.

Note
Graphs are not available as a table.

Labs
To view patient information from the Labs component in the Nursing Communication MPages in graph formatting select the name of the lab you want to view. The MPages view displays individual numeric lab information as a graph. Close the graph when you are finished reviewing the patient information.
