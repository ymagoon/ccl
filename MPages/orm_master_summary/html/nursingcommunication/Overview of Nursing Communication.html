MPages Help Pages : Overview of Nursing Communication
This page last changed on Apr 12, 2010 by MB9512.

This MPage provides a general summary for patients with the encounter type of Inpatient and can be opened from PowerChart (PowerChart.exe). It assists in communication between clinicians, specifically nursing, by providing a summary of patient information for change of care (shift change or change of care).

Note
The Nursing Communication MPage is not a replacement to the Patient Access List (PAL).

The Nursing Communication MPage is organized using the SBAR communication formatting. It contains the Situation/Background, Assessment, and Recommendations tabs that display different patient information.

  • Situation and Background: You can customize this tab to display patient information regarding their background history and personalized medical situation.
  • Assessment: You can customize this tab to display current patient status information (for example, assessments, vital signs, reports, or intake and output information).
  • Recommendations: You can customize this tab to display patient plan information (for example, discharge plan).
