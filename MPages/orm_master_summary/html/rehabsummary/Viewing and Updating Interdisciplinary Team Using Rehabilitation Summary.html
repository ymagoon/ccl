Once you have displayed a patient and clicked the Pulmonary Summary MPages tab in PowerChart, the Pulmonary Summary MPages view is displayed. To add documentation, complete the following steps:

  1. Search for the desired encounter pathway you want to document against. To complete this step, click "+Add" on the Documents component to launch the Documentation tab in PowerChart®.
