MPages Help Pages : Overview of Rehabilitation Summary
This page last changed on Jun 20, 2011 by JP3026.

The Rehabilitation Summary MPages view provides specific rehabilitation information for the Physical Therapist, Occupational Therapist, Speech-Language Pathologist, Nursing, Physician, and other disciplines to help in clinical decision making related to patient rehabilitation. It aids in rehabilitative care by providing pertinent supportive information. For example, different goals and plans can be established and the MPages view allows the therapist to view treatment results.
