R1 MPages Help Pages : Adding Education Items in Pregnancy Summary
This page last changed on Apr 12, 2010 by CH8732.

To add education items from the Education and Counseling Component of the Pregnancy Summary Mpage complete the following steps:

  1. Click the Add button (+Add) next to the Education and Counseling heading.
  2. The system opens the defined PowerForm.
    Note
    You can define the PowerForm in Cerner Bedrock. For more information see the All About Pregnancy Summary Mpage Reference Page.
  3. Enter the appropriate education information.
  4. Save and close the PowerForm.
