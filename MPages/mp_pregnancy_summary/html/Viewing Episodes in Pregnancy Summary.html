R1 MPages Help Pages : Viewing Episodes in Pregnancy Summary
This page last changed on May 24, 2010 by JS015791.

The Fetal Monitoring section displays data related to the status of the fetus and provides a link to the FetaLink component.  If licensed and applicable, the FetaLink component displays the monitoring data of the fetus.

You can view up to three fetal monitoring episodes. Complete the following steps to view the detailed information about an episode.

  1. Select the episode you want to view.
  2. Click the Show link. The CAMM archive in FetaLink is displayed.
