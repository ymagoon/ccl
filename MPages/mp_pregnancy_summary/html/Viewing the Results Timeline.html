R1 MPages Help Pages : Viewing the Results Timeline
This page last changed on May 21, 2010 by JS015791.

The Results Timeline provides a graphical presentation of key clinical events that have occurred during the pregnancy. You can view the visits, orders, lab results, and ultrasounds for each trimester. The events are displayed by gestational age in weeks. Each trimester is shaded differently. Vertical lines mark Today, Estimated Delivery Date, and Delivery Date on the timeline.

Icons are displayed to mark the following events:

Visit
Lab
Ultrasound

To view additional information, position the cursor over an icon to open up a window with further details.
