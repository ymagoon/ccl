R1 MPages Help Pages : Viewing Assessments
This page last changed on May 25, 2010 by JS015791.

The Assessments (and initial Physical Exam) section pulls in appropriate client-defined results for accessible viewing. The information displayed in this section reflects the most current information consistent with the "as of" (last refreshed time), as indicated in the top right corner of the main PowerChart window.

The Secondary Assessment section behaves identically to the Assessments and initial Physical Exam section, with the values displayed being different than what is included within the initial assessments section.

Your site's specifications determines the display values and intended uses for each section.

To view the Assessments components, complete the following steps.

  1. Open the patient chart to the Pregnancy Summary mPage of a patient with an active pregnancy.
  2. You can view the Initial Assessment detail by expanding the component within the mPage.
