R1 MPages Help Pages : Viewing Notes and Documents in Pregnancy Summary
This page last changed on May 04, 2010 by TS011807.

To view notes from the Documents component in the Pregnancy Summary Mpage complete the following steps:

  1. Select the documented date link that displays next to the document name you want to view.
  2. The system displays the note.

You can modify the note by entering your updates in the box provided. The system will save and display your new comments.
