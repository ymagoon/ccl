The Visits component displays the previous five patient visits, and up to five future visits. The previous visits are displayed first and in reverse chronological order, followed by any future visits in chronological order. Hovering over a visit will display the visit reason (Video will be updated shortly.)

Clicking the Visits section header will take you to the Patient Demographic component in the chart, where I can look at the Visit List and view additional information. Then return to the summary page by clicking in the Table of Contents.
Component Details

Heading

  • Visits (n previous / n future)
    n = total number of previous visits and total number of future visits
  • Component heading will link to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Visits are grouped into sub-headings of Past and Future
  • Date of visit
  • Type of visit
  • Location of visit

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: Previous 5 visits and up to 5 future visits
  • Sort Order: display past visits first in reverse chronological order, then display future visits in chronological order
  • Truncate / Wrap: text will wrap if it exceeds the column width
  • Scrolling: client defined

Hover

  • Visit Reason
