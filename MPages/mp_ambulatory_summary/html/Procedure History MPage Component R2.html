Overview

The Procedure History component displays all procedures for the lifetime of the patient sorted alphabetically. The year of the procedure is displayed to the right of the name.

Positioning the mouse over a procedure displays the procedure name, date, status, provider, location, and any comments. Clicking the section header takes you to the History control in the chart. You can then click on the Procedure History tab.

Component Details

Heading

  • Procedures (n)
    n = total number of procedure history items
  • Component heading will link to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Display active procedure annotated display name, if it exists, otherwise the component displays the procedure name.
  • Display procedure date.

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: lifetime
  • Status: display all active procedure history items
  • Sort Order: alphabetical
  • Truncate / Wrap: text wraps if it exceeds the column width
  • Scrolling: client-defined

Hover

  • Procedure: followed by the name of the procedure.
  • Annotated Display: followed by the annotated display of the procedure.
  • Procedure Date: followed by the date of the procedure.
  • Status: followed by the status of the problem.
  • Provider: followed by the provider who performed the procedure.
  • Location: followed by the location of the procedure.
  • Comments: followed by all associated comments for the procedure.
