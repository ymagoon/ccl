The Patient Information component displays the patient's chief complaint, primary physician, emergency contact and phone number, advanced directive, and date of the last clinic visit. Chief complaint and advanced directive will populate from the Ambulatory Intake and Admission History PowerForms.

Positioning the mouse over the emergency phone number will display any additional phone numbers for that contact. Positioning the mouse over the last clinic visit date will display the dates of all clinic visits for the last 2 years. Clicking the section header will take you to the Patient Information component in the chart. I'll go back to the Ambulatory Summary screen by clicking in the Table of Contents. Most of the patient information fields are populated based on registration information, but the chief complaint and advanced directive come from PowerForm documentation. To demonstrate I'll got to Ad Hoc Charting and choose my ambulatory intake and history PowerForms. I'll chart my chief complaint as sore throat with coughing (sign the form). On the history form I will say yes for advanced directive, and they have a living will (sign the form). I close Ad Hoc Charting, refresh my screen, and you can see that my chief complaint has been updated and the advanced directive is updated.
Component Details

Heading

  • Patient Information
  • Component heading will link to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Chief Complaint
  • Primary Physician's Name
  • Emergency Contact's Name
  • Emergency Contact's Phone Number
  • Advanced Directive

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: pull historical data from lifetime and current information from admission data
  • Truncate / Wrap: text will wrap if it exceeds the column width

Hover

  • Hovering over Emergency # will display all phone numbers for the emergency contact
