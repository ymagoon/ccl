The Social History component displays all social history items for the lifetime of the patient.
Items are sorted alphabetically by category
Hovering over an item will display the name of the category and any details that were documented.

Clicking the section header will take you to the History control in the chart. From here I can select the Social History tab. I want to document social history so I'm going to add tobacco (click Add). I'll say it was past use, he smoked cigars, one a day for 10 years, starting at age 25 and ending at 35. Click OK. Now I'll return to the Ambulatory Summary by clicking in the Table of Contents. I refresh the screen, and my new social history items is documented.
Component Details

Heading

  • Social(n)
    n = total number of social history items
  • Component heading will link to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Display category name followed by the documented assessment

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: lifetime
  • Sort Order: by collating sequence and then alphabetical by category
  • Truncate / Wrap: text will wrap if it exceeds the column width
  • Scrolling: client defined

Hover

  • Category
  • Details
  • Last Updated Date/Time
  • Last Updated By
  • Comments
