Overview

The Outstanding Orders component displays all outstanding orders for the patient. This could be active orders, or completed orders that were never cosigned. It looks back two years and across all encounters. Hovering over an order displays the order name, details, date, and status.

Clicking the section header takes you to PowerOrders in the chart. From here you can write new orders or take action on existing orders before returning to the summary page. Returen to the summary page by clicking in the table of contents.
