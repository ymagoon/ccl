The Health Maintenance component displays all overdue expectations and expectations that are due up to one year in the future. Items are displayed in chronological order. There is no hover functionality at this time.

Clicking the section header will take you to the Health Maintenance component in the chart. From here I can perform any of the normal Health Maintenance activities. I'll mark the prostate cancer screening as performed elsewhere. Click OK. Now I'll return to the Ambulatory Summary by clicking in the Table of Contents. I'll refresh the screen, and I can see that my previous prostate cancer screening has been removed and the next one is due one year from today.
Component Details

Heading

  • Health Maintenance (n Overdue / n Due)
    n = total number of due or overdue health maintenance items
  • Component heading will link to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Display the health maintenance item name
  • Display the date due or overdue, right justified on the same line as the item

Data Format

  • Look Forward Range: one year
  • Status: all due or overdue items
  • Sort Order: chronological order by due date

Hover

  • Nothing at this time
