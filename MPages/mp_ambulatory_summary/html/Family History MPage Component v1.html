This page last changed on Jan 15, 2010 by SM015827.

The Family History component displays all positive family history items for the lifetime of the patient. The family member having a positive history for the condition is displayed to the right of the condition.
Items are sorted alphabetically.
There is currently no hover function for Family History.

Clicking on the section header will take you to the History control in the chart. From here I can select the Family History tab. I want to document a new family history, so I click Add. We'll document cleft palate for father and brother. For brother we want some additional details. He was born in November of last year, this was present at birth, and click OK. Click OK again to document. Now I'll return to the Ambulatory Summary by clicking in the Table of Contents. I refresh the screen, and my new family history items are displayed.
Component Details

Heading

  • Family (n)
    n = total number of family history items
  • Component heading will link to appropriate component in the chart

Content

  • Display condition name
  • Display family member having a positive history of the condition

Data Format

  • Look Back Range: lifetime
  • Status: display all positive conditions
  • Sort Order: alphabetical by condition
  • Truncate / Wrap: text will wrap if it exceeds the column width
  • Scrolling: client defined

Hover

  • None at this time
