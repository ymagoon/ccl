This page last changed on Aug 17, 2010 by RP018820.

The Health Maintenance component displays all overdue expectations and expectations that are due up to one year in the future. Items are grouped together by Overdue or Due status and then in chronological order. This component has no hover functionality at this time.

Clicking the section header takes you to the Health Maintenance component in the chart. From here you can perform any of the normal Health Maintenance activities.

Component Details

Heading

  • Health Maintenance (n Overdue / n Due)
    n = total number of overdue or due health maintenance items displayed
  • Allow link from component heading to PowerChart tab to be defined.

Content

  • Display the health maintenance item/expectation in the first column
  • Display the date due or overdue on the same line with the health maintenance item/expectation in the second column

Data Format

  • Look Forward Range: one year
  • Scrolling: client-defined
  • Status: all due or overdue items
  • Sort Order: chronological order by due date
  • Scrolling: Client defined.

Hover

  • Nothing at this time
