Overview

The purpose of this component is to provide you the ability to do the following directly from the summary:

  • View a child's growth chart information
  • Access the growth chart tab in one click
  • Access the appropriate PowerForms to document growth information
Component Details

Heading

  • CDC Growth Chart
  • Allow component heading to be modified.
  • Display text to the right of label: "Last 2 years".

Content

  • Default the following growth chart results:
    • Weight
    • Height
    • BMI
    • Head Circ

Data Format

  • Look back range: Last two years (not encounter specific)
  • Displays three columns of results:
    • First column - most current result.
    • Second column - previous result.
    • Third column - previous result.
  • Scrolling: Client defined.

Hover

  • Display "Age": Followed by the age of the patient at the time of the result.
  • Display "Result dt/tm": Followed by the date and time of the result.
  • Display "Result": Followed by the result value and unit of measure.
  • Display "Percentile": Followed by the percentile the patient compares to other people of this age and result.
  • Display "Z-score": Followed by the z-score.
