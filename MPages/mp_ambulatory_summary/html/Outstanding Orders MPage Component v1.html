R1 MPages Help Pages : Outstanding Orders MPage Component v1
This page last changed on Jan 15, 2010 by SM015827.

The Outstanding Orders component displays all outstanding orders for the patient. This could be active orders, or completed orders that were never cosigned. It looks back two years and across all encounters. Positioning the mouse over an order will display the order name, details, date, and status.

Clicking the section header will take you to PowerOrders in the chart. From here I can write new orders or take action on existing orders before returning to the summary page. To demonstrate the outstanding orders component I'll quickly place a new lab order. Now I'll return to the summary page by clicking in the table of contents. I refresh the screen, and my active lab order is displayed.
