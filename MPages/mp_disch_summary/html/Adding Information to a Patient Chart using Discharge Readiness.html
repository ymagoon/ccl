R1 MPages Help Pages : Adding Information to a Patient Chart using Discharge Readiness
This page last changed on Dec 14, 2010 by JP3026.

You can post directly to PowerChartÂ® from the Discharge Summary MPages view. You have several options for entering information into the patient chart listed below. Follow one or more of the options to enter the appropriate data:

Option One:

  1. Navigate to the Discharge Summary.
  2. Click the title of a selected component to launch the appropriate PowerChart tab. The PowerChart tab is used to enter data generally.

Option Two:

  1. Navigate to the Discharge Summary MPages view.
  2. If a "+Add" is displayed to the right of the component title, you can select it to launch the PowerChart module used to enter data for that component. Some examples are listed below:
    1. Patient Education component - "+Add" launches the Patient Education window where the patient education instructions can be selected.
    2. Follow Up component - "+Add" launches the Follow-Up module for entering follow-up information.
    3. Documents component - "+Add" launches the PowerNote Documentation tab for selecting the specific encounter pathway.

Option Three:

  1. Navigate to the MPages Summary view.
  2. If multiple items have been defined in the Bedrock Wizard, a drop down arrow is displayed to the right of the "+Add" option for a specific component.
  3. To display the list of specific PowerNotes, click on the drop down arrow icon next to the component title. From there, you can directly launch the notes.

When you click "+Add", the system can also launches one of the PowerChart components listed below for entering and modifying data:

  • Medication Reconciliation
  • Allergies
  • Discharge Order
