Kidney Disease Improving Global Outcomes Guideline (KDIGO) for Acute Kidney Injury (AKI):
Stage 1: Serum creatinine: 1.5-1.9 times baseline (7 days) or >=0.3 mg/dl increase (48hr)
Stage 2: Serum creatinine: 2.0-2.9 times baseline (7 days)
Stage 3: Serum creatinine: 3.0 or higher times baseline (7 days)
